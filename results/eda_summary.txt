# Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) Summary

## 1. Stock Closing Price Trends
- Apple (AAPL), Microsoft (MSFT), Netflix (NFLX), and Google (GOOG) stock prices were analyzed.
- **Trend Observations:**
  - AAPL showed a steady increase with minor fluctuations.
  - NFLX displayed high volatility compared to others.
  - MSFT and GOOG followed similar growth trends.
- The closing price trend was visualized in `closing_price_trends.png`.

## 2. Correlation Analysis
- Strong positive correlations between **Open, High, Low, and Close** prices.
- **Volume** showed a weak negative correlation with price.
- The correlation matrix heatmap is saved in `correlation_matrix.png`.

## 3. Moving Averages & Volatility
- 50-day and 200-day moving averages were calculated.
- **Volatility Analysis:** Netflix had the highest standard deviation in returns.
- The volatility pattern was useful in understanding market risks.
